Tutorial
========


This part of the device programming activity can be done as soon as the boards are in your hands.
Watch a few videos (curated from the web), learn about the general programming approach, and get the LEDs to blink.

The debugging section has some more advanced (but optional) material in it that may take longer, depending on your understanding of your own computer.
But the rest should take in total about an hour to try.
